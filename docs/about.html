About UTA MACS

Building a community where every resident feels safe, heard, and at home.

Who We Are

Urban Trilla Apartment
Mutually Aided Cooperative Society

UTA MACS is the resident welfare association for the Urban Trilla Apartment complex located at Survey Number 425/2/1, Kondakal Village, Rajendranagar Mandal, Ranga Reddy District, Hyderabad — 500086.

Registered under the Telangana Mutually Aided Cooperative Societies Act, we are a democratic, resident-led organization that manages the maintenance, security, and community development of our 500+ family complex.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Transparency

We publish all financial statements, meeting minutes, and decisions openly so every resident is fully informed.

Community First

Every decision is made with the collective well-being of all 500+ families in mind — not individual interests.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in maintenance, security, and community services that residents deserve.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the growth of our community.

2015

Foundation

UTA MACS was officially registered as a Mutually Aided Cooperative Society under the Telangana MACS Act, bringing together the first 100 families.

2018

Infrastructure Growth

Major upgrades including installation of CCTV surveillance, 24/7 security, community hall renovation, and establishment of the maintenance fund.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched the community website, introduced digital notice boards, online payment tracking, and streamlined communication channels.

2024

New Milestones

Crossed 500 family milestone, upgraded common area facilities, planted 200 trees as part of the green initiative, and launched resident portal development.
