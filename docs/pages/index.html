About UTA MACS

Urban Trilla Apartment Owners Mutually Aided Cooperative Maintenance Society Limited represents residents of Urban Trilla Apartments, fostering community welfare and cooperative governance.

Our Vision

To create a harmonious and sustainable community where residents work together for mutual benefit and shared prosperity.

Our Mission

Provide excellent maintenance services, promote community engagement, and ensure transparent governance for all residents.
