About the Association

Urban Trilla Apartment Owners Mutually Aided Cooperative Maintenance Society Limited represents the residents of Urban Trilla Apartments located at Kondakal, Shankarpalle, Ranga Reddy District, Telangana, India.

Registration No: TG/RRD/MACS/2026-15/FOW & M
Certificate issued on 10-02-2026.

Our association is dedicated to cooperative governance, resident welfare, infrastructure upkeep, and community events for apartment owners and families in the Urban Trilla community.

Why join?

  • Reliable building and shared-area maintenance support
  • Clear governance through elected member representation
  • Community events, notices, and resident-first communication
