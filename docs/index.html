About the Society

URBAN TRILLA APARTMENT OWNERS MUTUALLY AIDED COOPERATIVE MAINTENANCE SOCIETY LIMITED is a registered cooperative society under the Government of Telangana Cooperation Department, Ranga Reddy District.

Registration No: TG/RRD/MACS/2026-15/FOW & M
Certificate issued on 10-02-2026.

Based at Survey Number 425/2/1, Kondakkal(V), Shankarpalle(M), Ranga Reddy(D), 501203, our society supports apartment owners with cooperative maintenance, governance, and community growth.
